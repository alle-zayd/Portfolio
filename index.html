Mobile App

PumpScout Davao

PumpScout Davao is a mobile application designed to eliminate fuel price non-transparency and refueling inefficiencies for motorists in Davao City. The platform integrates crowdsourced fuel rates, real-time IoT infrared sensor hardware tracking, and machine learning to map out gas stations, analyze live queue traffic, and predict price trends. Its primary objectives are to track user proximity to stations, visualize color-coded traffic congestion states, and generate optimal, fuel-efficient routing choices.
